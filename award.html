Award and linkages

Discover the exciting awards and invaluable linkages available at Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center. Our prestigious awards celebrate innovation and entrepreneurship, while our strong network of partners provides invaluable connections and opportunities for growth. Unleash your potential and propel your business to new heights with our award programs and strategic linkages.

Our awards:

are designed to celebrate innovation, creativity, and the impact you're making in your industry. By participating in our award programs, you have the opportunity to gain visibility, showcase your accomplishments, and gain recognition for your hard work. Whether it's an award for product innovation, social impact, or overall business excellence, our awards provide a platform to highlight your achievements and attract attention from potential investors, partners, and customers.
In addition to the awards, we also provide strategic linkages to help you grow your business.

Strategic linkages

We have a wide network of partners, including investors, industry experts, mentors, and other entrepreneurs, who can offer valuable support and connections. These linkages can open doors to new opportunities, collaborations, and funding sources, helping you accelerate your business growth and expand your reach.

Through our strategic linkages:

you'll have access to workshops, networking events, and mentorship programs that can enhance your skills, broaden your knowledge, and enable you to learn from experienced professionals. This ecosystem of support and collaboration is designed to empower you with the resources and connections you need to thrive in the competitive business landscape.
