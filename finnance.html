Access to Finance

Open the doors to financial possibilities with our Access to Finance program at Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center. We understand that securing funding is essential for entrepreneurs like you to turn your ideas into reality.

Our Access to Finance program:

Offers a range of opportunities, including grants, loans, and investment options. We work closely with you to understand your specific funding requirements and guide you through the application process, ensuring that you have the best chance of securing the financial support you need.
