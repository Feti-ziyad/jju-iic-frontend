About Us

Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center is a leading hub for innovation, entrepreneurship, and transformative thinking. Located at Jigjiga University, our center serves as a catalyst for driving economic growth, societal progress, and positive change in the region. At Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center, we foster collaboration, interdisciplinary partnerships, and knowledge sharing. We believe in the power of diverse perspectives and aim to create an inclusive community where individuals can connect, collaborate, and co-create.

Our Mission

At Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center, our mission is to fuel innovation and empower entrepreneurs to make a lasting impact. We provide a supportive ecosystem that nurtures creativity, fosters collaboration, and offers tailored support for startups. Through comprehensive programs, mentorship, and industry partnerships, we aim to facilitate the development of groundbreaking ideas and transform them into successful ventures. Our mission is to drive economic growth, create employment opportunities, and contribute to the overall prosperity of the community through entrepreneurship and innovation.

Our Vision

At Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center, our vision is To ignite a culture of innovation and empower individuals to drive positive change through entrepreneurship. We envision a future where Jigjiga University Innovation Incubation Center serves as a catalyst for transformative ideas, fostering a dynamic ecosystem that fuels creativity, collaboration, and societal impact. By providing comprehensive support, resources, and mentorship, we aim to empower entrepreneurs to turn their visions into successful ventures that contribute to economic growth, social progress, and a better future for all. Together, let's ignite innovation, shape the future, and inspire lasting change.
