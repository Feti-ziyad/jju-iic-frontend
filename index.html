What We Offer

Training

Our training programs empower aspiring innovators like you to bring groundbreaking ideas to life. With expert trainers, collaborative environments, and a focus on creativity and resilience, our center equips you with the skills and mindset needed for entrepreneurial success.

Learn More

Mentoring

Our mentoring program is designed to provide you with personalized guidance and support on your entrepreneurial journey. We believe that having a mentor is an invaluable resource that can help you navigate the challenges of building and growing your business.

Learn More

Award and Linkage

we offer exciting opportunities through our awards program and linkages. Our awards recognize exceptional achievements in innovation, providing recognition and opening doors to new opportunities, and our linkages connect you with industry experts, investors, and collaborators.

Learn More

Consultancy

Our experienced consultants offer practical insights and solutions to help you navigate the challenges of innovation and entrepreneurship. Whether through one-on-one sessions or group workshops, we are committed to helping you turn your vision into a reality.

Learn More

Access to Finance

At the Innovation Incubation Center, we provide comprehensive support to help you access the finance you need for your innovation. Through our extensive network of investors and financial institutions, we connect you with funding opportunities that align with your venture.

Learn More

Market Linkage and Networking

We provide valuable market linkage opportunities and a vibrant networking ecosystem. Our extensive network connects you with industry experts, potential customers, and strategic partners, while our networking events and workshops help you build meaningful relationships.
